FAQ How to get login credintials ?

Username & Password will be provided by the admin

 How to recover the password when the password is forgotten ?

User can recover his password through your SLIIT email

 How to check the available balance tickets ?

Click on the pie cahrt provided in the home page, pie chart will provide the balance ticket

How loan is provided ?

If there are no balance tickets you can get maximum of two tickets but extra 10 rupees must be paid to get the loan again, previous all balance should be settled

How to make the payment

You have to give the amount, name, SLIIT id & then your have to click on make payment button. Then there will be some payment option. You have to choose one option for the make payment

 How to get the current location & bus location

Go to the bus location page in the more option and you can check the current location and bus location
